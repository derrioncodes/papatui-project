PRODUCT EXPERIENCE

Are all of your products dermatologist tested?

Yes - all PAPATUI™ products are Dermatologist Tested

Are your products PETA Vegan and Cruelty Free?

Absolutely. All of our products are cruelty-free and vegan. PAPATUI™ is a member of PETA's Beauty Without Bunnies program - none of our products and ingredients are tested on animals, and we always choose vegan alternatives.

Are your products recyclable?

Yes, all PAPATUI™ products are made with the environment in mind. All local recycling programs are different, so please look into your local curbside recycling program to sort your products for your local recycling team.

Does PAPATUI™ use Post Consumer Recycled (PCR) Plastic?

Yes - wherever possible. We make sure at least 30% of the plastic we use is PCR.

Are your products Clean?

At PAPATUI™, we strive to create products that are as clean as possible while high performing. We look to use the cleanest ingredients possible first and foremost as long as we didn't sacrifice efficacy.

Where are your products made?

Our PAPATUI™ Tattoo Stick, Tattoo Balm, Antiperspirant and Deodorant are made in Canada. All other PAPATUI™ products are made in the USA with globally sourced ingredients and components.

Are your products safe for use by children?

PAPATUI™ products are considered safe for everyone in your family. For any concerns, please contact your pediatrician.

Do your products contain any pthalates?

No - all PAPATUI™ products are all Phthalate-free.

Do your products contain sulfates?

No - sulfates can cause redness and dry itchy skin by removing too much oil and washing off the skin’s protective barrier. They can cause reactions for sensitive skin. We do not have them in our products.

Do your products contain dyes?

No - PAPATUI™ products do not contain dyes.

Do your products contain silicones?

WPAPATUI™ products are Free Of D4, D5, and D6 Silicones, Silicones that may create a barrier on the skin that could trap moisture and impede the skin's natural ability to "breathe." This is particularly relevant for those with oily or acne-prone skin. These ingredients are known for forming a film on the skin, and some individuals worry about potential product buildup. People with sensitive skin may choose silicone-free products to help minimize the risk of irritation or allergic reactions, as silicones may be problematic for some individuals especially if they use a variety of skincare products.

How do I use the eye roller ball on the Awakening Eye Gel?

Out of the box, you will want to prime the roller ball. Press the roller ball gently on back of your hand, gently squeeze the tube, and roll back and forth until you will see and feel the gel. If the roller ball seems stuck, just gently push it down with your finger or on the back of your hand and then gently squeeze and roll.

Once the liquid begins to dispense, gently roll back and forth underneath eyes. Once you feel there's a nice amount of liquid, use your ring finger to smooth it out

Can I use the Tattoo Balm or Tattoo Stick on a brand new tattoo?

It is recommended to first use ointment with fresh tattoos, and once the skin has healed (no broken/cracked skin), you may begin using PAPATUI™ Tattoo Balm or PAPATUI™ Tattoo Stick.

Is it common to experience issues transitioning from antiperspirant to aluminum-free deodorant?

Antiperspirant-deodorant is an aluminum based product designed to prevent sweat (perspirant) and odor. Deodorant is aluminum free and created solely to mask armpit odor but not stop you from sweating. Some people experience concerns transitioning from antiperspirant to deodorant and need up to 30 days for their body to regulate itself and sweat naturally again. There are a multitude of sources online that highlight Natural Deodorant Detox. Whether sweat & odor protection or just odor protection is right for you, PAPATUI™ has a high performing product for you.

PAPATUI™ Deodorant is proven 48-hour odor protection; PAPATUI™ Antiperspirant is proven 48-hour sweat and odor protection. Any additional questions, be sure to consult a healthcare professional.

I cut myself while shaving my underarms. Can I still use your antiperspirant?

We do not recommend using our product on broken skin. We recommend that you wait until your skin has healed to use our product.

Does the Nourishing Shampoo+Conditioner treat dandruff?

PAPATUI™ Nourishing Shampoo + Conditioner is amazing at lifting dirt and oil trapped in the hair and on the scalp, while being kind and gentle on the scalp - but it doesn't treat dandruff. If you need to fight dandruff or prevent dandruff-forming microbes, we recomment reaching out to your healthcare professional for their advice for the best anti-dandruff product for you.

Is the Nourishing Shampoo+Conditioner safe for color-treated hair?

Sulfates and other harsh ingredients are known to damage hair - especially color-treated hair. Our PAPATUI™ Nourishing Shampoo + Conditioner is formualted without sulfates and helps your color-treated hair stay moisturized, which can help preserve your hair's new color.

Is the Nourishing Shampoo+Conditioner safe for sensitive scalps?

PAPATUI™ Nourishing Shampoo + Conditioner is gentle for those who have sensitive scalp.
