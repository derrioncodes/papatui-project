FAQS

General

Where can I buy PAPATUI™ products?

PAPATUI™ products can be purchased on papatui.com, Target.com, and inside your local Target store (starting to roll-out from March 10th).

Do you have a consumer or professional sampling program?

Unfortunately, we do not have a consumer or professional sampling program at this time.

Do you have gift cards?

Unfortunately, we do not offer any gift cards at this time. Please check our website www.papatui.com for future availability.

Can women use PAPATUI™ products?

Although our products are formulated with men in mind they can be used and enjoyed by anyone.

ORDERS

Do you ship to Canada?

We're working to ship to Canada and as soon as we do you'll be the first to know

Where do you ship?

We currently only ship within the United States. We do not ship to the Virgin Islands, Puerto Rico, or Canada.

Do you offer free shipping?

As a special launch treat we are offering free shipping on all orders $40 or more

When will my order ship?

Papatui.com orders are typically processed within 2-4 business days (we don't ship on weekends or major holidays). Orders placed by 1 PM EST Monday through Friday will be processed the same day. Then, it's passed to our shipping carriers. Once the carrier accepts and scans your package, the tracking information sent via email will be updated and give an ETA on when you can expect your package to arrive.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard and American Express. We also accept Paypal, Google Pay, Shop Pay and Venmo. We are unable to accept prepaid gift cards, checks, or money orders

I have a question about my order, who can I talk to?

You've got questions, we've got answers. Please reach out to us at support@papatui.com for any order related questions and we'll be happy to help. We are available Monday - Friday from 8am-5pm EST

Can I cancel my order?

After you place an order our team gets working on it right away. Because of this, we cannot guarantee that we'll be able to cancel your order once it's been placed, but we'll do our best. Please reach out to us at support@papatui.com

What do I do if a product I want is out of stock?

If you click “Notify me when available”, you will receive an email when the product is back in stock. Please note this will not be available during promotional periods. Please check back often as we will be restocking on an on-going basis. Please also note that our team is not able to provide restock timing for products that are unavailable. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Then, it's passed to our shipping carriers. Once the carrier accepts and scans your package, the tracking information sent via email will be updated and give an ETA on when you can expect your package to arrive.

Do you offer returns?

We love our products and hope you do too. However, if you purchased your products through papatui.com and your order is not to your liking, please email us at support@papatui.com for more information.

If you did not purchase your products through papatui.com and instead at Target or Target.com, we recommend reaching out directly to Target for a refund.

How do I create a subscription order/account?

When placing an order, select the “Subscribe” option on the product page. This will allow you to select the frequency in which you want that item delivered. Once you have successfully completed that order and checked out, you are enrolled and have created a subscription account.

How do I manage/update my subscription?

To manage your subscription, log into your account on papatui.com and navigate to your account page. At the top under MY ACCOUNT click on the link MANAGE SUBSCRIPTIONS.

There you will be able to manage any item you have on subscription, skip a delivery or reschedule an upcoming delivery
